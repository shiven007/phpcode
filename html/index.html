Welcome to Kalista Global

We are a global re/insurance and financial advisory company with excellent connectivity within the investment banking, insurance products and equity markets.

We are

actively involved in the alternative reinsurance space

Kalista is actively involved with the insurance-linked securities (ILS*) market. The principals of Kalista believe that this segment of the industry is poised for significant growth as reinsurers, governments and large corporate buyers of insurance continue to access capital market solutions as an alternative to traditional risk transfer products.

We were

founded in 2011

Kalista was set up in 2011 to handle a diverse range of reinsurance products for a sophisticated international client base; the principals of Kalista have been at the forefront of the convergence of the insurance / reinsurance and capital markets arena being involved in a number of high profile and ground breaking financial structures..

We are

Experts at structuring bespoke deals

We are specialists in structuring bespoke deals for unique underlying risks previously considered difficult to underwrite or "uninsurable" - ranging from complex Property Catastrophe exposures, to Space, Oil & Gas, and even Revenue Protection for Project Financings.
